Portal Boundary

Institutional staff workspace for the shared recruitment engine.

Internal access remains separate from the public applicant portal while still using the same backend, one database, and one workflow engine for Plantilla and COS recruitment.

Protected internal roles only

Secretariat, HRM Chief, HRMPSB Member, Appointing Authority, and System Administrator accounts use this sign-in page.

Level-aware routing remains enforced

Level 1 routes to Secretariat, while Level 2 routes to HRM Chief unless a controlled override is explicitly granted and logged.

Applicant functions stay public-facing

Public submission, OTP verification, receipt, and status lookup remain under the separate applicant portal.

Operational module surface

The internal workspace supports dashboard, case handling, workflow routing, evaluation records, evidence controls, audit visibility, export, and system administration under one institutional layout.
