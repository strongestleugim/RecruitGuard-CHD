# RecruitGuard-CHD Modules

## 1. Identity and Access Control
Manages internal authentication, session control, and role-based permissions.

## 2. Recruitment Entry and Vacancy Management
Manages Plantilla vacancies and COS openings/pooling entries.

## 3. Applicant Intake and OTP Verification
Handles applicant-facing submission, OTP verification, and application finalization.

## 4. Recruitment Case Management and Workflow Engine
Creates recruitment cases and enforces stage-based workflow progression.

## 5. Branch-Aware and Level-Aware Routing
Routes cases according to Plantilla/COS branch and Level 1/Level 2 rules.

## 6. Document Review and Qualification Screening
Handles completeness review, qualification checking, and screening outcomes.

## 7. Examination Management
Handles exam-related records where applicable.

## 8. Interview and Rating Management
Handles interview scheduling, rating entry, and finalized interview outputs.

## 9. Deliberation and Decision Support
Handles consolidation, deliberation records, ranking, and CAR where applicable.

## 10. Decision and Approval Handling
Handles final decision recording and decision-side actions.

## 11. Notification Management
Handles submission, selection, non-selection, and checklist/deadline notifications.

## 12. Appointment and Contract Completion
Handles post-selection completion within recruitment scope only.

## 13. Evidence Vault and Record Management
Stores files, metadata, versions, and searchable records.

## 14. Audit Logging and Traceability
Maintains recruitment-grade audit logs and traceability.

## 15. Evidence Export and Integrity Verification
Generates controlled export bundles and integrity verification outputs.
