Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about applying for positions at DOH–CHD CALABARZON.

{# ── Section: Applying ─────────────────────────────────────── #}

Applying for a Position

No. Applications are submitted without creating an account. You only need a valid email address. We will send a 6-digit verification code to that email to confirm your identity before your application is finalized.

The required documents are listed on each position's detail page before you start applying. In general, you will need:

  • Personal Data Sheet (CS Form 212, Revised 2017)
  • Updated Resume or Curriculum Vitae
  • Scanned copies of your educational certificates and transcript of records
  • Certificate of Eligibility (for Plantilla positions)
  • Service records or employment certificates (if applicable)

Documents should be saved as PDF, JPG, or PNG files, with a maximum size of 5 MB per file.

Yes. You may apply for multiple open positions. Each position requires a separate application submission. You will receive a unique Application ID for each one.

Once you click "Submit My Application" and it is confirmed, your application cannot be edited online. If you need to make a correction or withdraw, please email us at recruitment@chd4a.doh.gov.ph and include your Application ID.

{# ── Section: Email Verification ────────────────────────────── #}

Email Verification

  1. Check your Spam or Junk Mail folder. The email comes from a government recruitment address and may be filtered.
  2. Make sure the email address you entered is correct — go back and check.
  3. Click "Resend Code" on the verification page to request a new code. You can resend once every 60 seconds.
  4. If you still don’t receive it after a few minutes, try a different email address and start a new application.

The 6-digit code is valid for a limited time after it is sent. If it expires, click "Resend Code" to get a fresh one.

Yes. On the verification page, click "Go back to edit your information" at the bottom. This will return you to the application form where you can correct your email address. You will need to re-upload your documents.

{# ── Section: After Submitting ──────────────────────────────── #}

After Submitting

Your Application ID is a unique reference number (e.g., RG-2024-00001) assigned to your application the moment you submit. It is shown on the confirmation page and sent to your email. Keep it — you will need it to track your application status.

Go to the Track Application page and enter your Application ID and email address. The portal will show your current status in plain language.

This means our recruitment team has sent your application back because something needs to be addressed — for example, a missing document or additional information is required. Check your email for instructions. If you did not receive an email, contact us at recruitment@chd4a.doh.gov.ph with your Application ID.

Processing timelines vary depending on the position and the number of applicants. Government recruitment follows Civil Service Commission guidelines, which typically involve document review, evaluation, and approval stages. You will be contacted by our team if you are shortlisted or if any action is needed on your part.
